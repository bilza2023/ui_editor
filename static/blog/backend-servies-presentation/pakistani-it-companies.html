Pakistan IT Industry

Pakistan's IT sector has witnessed significant growth, with exports reaching $3.2 billion in FY 2023-24, marking a 24% increase from the previous year. The industry is projected to grow at a rate of 7.31% annually, aiming to reach $3.5 billion by 2029.
