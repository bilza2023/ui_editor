What is SaaS?

Software as a Service (SaaS) is a cloud-based software delivery model where applications are accessed over the internet. It eliminates the need for installation, hosting, or server maintenance by the customer.

SaaS is profitable because it offers recurring income through subscriptions, low per-client maintenance, and massive scalability. With one codebase and infrastructure, the service can support thousands of clients.

Providers can manage upgrades, security, and features centrally, reducing deployment costs and improving consistency across the board.
