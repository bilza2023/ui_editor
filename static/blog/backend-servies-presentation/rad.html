Rapid Application Development (RAD)

Rapid Application Development (RAD) is a method to build business systems without traditional software development. Instead, it uses powerful, ready-made tools like Notion, Zapier, Airtable, and others to deliver a working solution fast — sometimes in hours.

This approach is perfect when you want to:

  • 🚀 Quickly test a business idea without heavy investment
  • 🛠 Build a "working demo" before committing to full software
  • 📊 Automate daily tasks like client follow-ups, emails, form handling
  • 💡 Convert business workflows into digital processes with drag-and-drop simplicity

How a Small Business Becomes a System (With Notion)

  • 📁 Track Inventory: Create product tables, filter by stock, track vendors.
  • 📅 Client Scheduling: Add timelines, projects, and follow-up reminders.
  • 📨 Lead Management: Capture leads from forms and move them through pipelines.
  • ⚙️ Automation with Zapier: Auto-send emails, create tasks, or update Google Sheets in response to triggers.
  • 📲 Build It in a Day: A working system with data, forms, and automation — no coding!

RAD is ideal for small business owners, consultants, and startups who need results now. Once the business proves itself, the same system can be rebuilt as full software.
