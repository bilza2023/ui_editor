🔁 Zapier – Workflow Automation

Zapier connects over 5,000 apps and automates tasks between them without writing code. We can help you use Zapier to:

  • ✅ Auto-send emails when a form is submitted
  • ✅ Create a Google Sheet row when a new lead is captured
  • ✅ Receive Slack/Discord alerts when your website gets a contact form submission
  • ✅ Backup files to Google Drive when uploaded to Dropbox
  • ✅ Post a tweet when a blog is published

  • 📦 EC2: Hosting apps on virtual servers

  • 🧠 Rekognition: Face/image recognition and analysis
